Tearing Down Barriers to a Healthier You!

Our program is designed to help you overcome both your physical and mental roadblocks to a healthy lifestyle with our proven meal plans, fitness routines, and our unique habit coaches.

It was created for a particular kind of person I know all too well, because I was one. Someone trying to fit in healthier options into a hectic day-to-day, but then gets overwhelmed. It's for the person who decides to make healthier choices, but then, overnight, their motivation vanishes. Mostly, it's for those of us who know we have it in us, but don't understand why we can't make lasting change, no matter how badly we wish to.

Our program wasn't made to lose weight, or even to learn to make healthy decisions. It was created to change who we are. We motivate individuals to become healthy a person, not act like one. We understand that being healthy doesn't begin with our stomachs, it begins with our mind. The key to all of this is to get your subconsious mind as motivated as your conscious mind. By focusing on what motivates you, at your core as an individual, you can get yourself to make the decisions you logically know you should make. All of this without relying on fleeting willpower or motivation.

Does this sound like you?

Health Roadmap

Step 1: Set Goals and Define Path

When signing up, you will take an assessment that defines your motivation style, fitness goals, and what you will need to do to get there. There's no way to hit a target if you're not even aiming, so this step is vital.

Benefit: Outlines the path of your journey, giving clarity.

Step 2: Receive and Follow Weekly Plan

Each week you will recieve a personal custom plan. This includes your weekly meal plan, fitnes routine, habit-building resources and activities, and any rewards you have earned previously.

Benefit: Offers the structure needed for major achieving goals.

Step 3: Monthly Review and Analysis

Each month you will receive a custom report tailored to your goals and motivation style. This will show you the progress you've made, and areas where improvements could be made.

Benefit: Ensures optimal motivation throughout the program.

Step 4: Quarterly Coach Meetings

While you can schedule coaching sessions throughout the program. Each quarter you will have a special meeting with your coach to discuss last quarter's performance and make a plan for the next.

Benefit: Gives you peace of mind that you are on the right track.
